## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Shiva Purana is dedicated to?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Brahma  
**D)** Devi  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Shiva Purana is primarily dedicated to Lord Shiva and his various aspects.

### Question 2
**Q:** Shiva Purana has how many samhitas?  
**A)** 5  
**B)** 6  
**C)** 7  
**D)** 8  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Shiva Purana traditionally contains seven samhitas (sections).

### Question 3
**Q:** Shiva's consort mentioned in the Purana is?  
**A)** Lakshmi  
**B)** Parvati  
**C)** Saraswati  
**D)** Ganga  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Goddess Parvati (also called Uma, Gauri) is described as Shiva's consort.

### Question 4
**Q:** Shiva's vehicle is?  
**A)** Garuda  
**B)** Nandi  
**C)** Peacock  
**D)** Lion  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Nandi, the sacred bull, is Shiva's vehicle and devoted follower.

### Question 5
