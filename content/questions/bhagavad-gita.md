# Bhagavad Gita - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Smriti (Epics, Laws, Sutras)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Bhagavad Gita is part of which epic?  
**A)** Ramayana  
**B)** Mahabharata  
**C)** Puranas  
**D)** Vedas  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Bhagavad Gita is part of the Mahabharata's Bhishma Parva.

### Question 2
**Q:** Who spoke Bhagavad Gita?  
**A)** Vyasa  
**B)** Arjuna  
**C)** Krishna  
**D)** Bhishma  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Lord Krishna spoke the Bhagavad Gita to Arjuna.

### Question 3
**Q:** How many chapters in Bhagavad Gita?  
**A)** 12  
**B)** 15  
**C)** 18  
**D)** 20  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Bhagavad Gita has 18 chapters.

### Question 4
**Q:** Gita was spoken on which battlefield?  
**A)** Panipat  
**B)** Kurukshetra  
**C)** Tarain  
**D)** Lanka  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Gita was revealed on the battlefield of Kurukshetra.

### Question 5
**Q:** Arjuna refused to fight because of?  
**A)** Fear  
**B)** Compassion for relatives  
**C)** Cowardice  
**D)** Injury  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Arjuna was conflicted about killing his relatives and teachers.

### Question 6
**Q:** Gita emphasizes which yoga primarily?  
**A)** Karma Yoga  
**B)** Bhakti Yoga  
**C)** Jnana Yoga  
**D)** All three  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Gita teaches all three paths: Karma, Bhakti, and Jnana Yoga.

### Question 7
**Q:** Who was Arjuna's charioteer?  
**A)** Bhima  
**B)** Krishna  
**C)** Yudhishthira  
**D)** Karna  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Lord Krishna served as Arjuna's charioteer during the war.

### Question 8
**Q:** Gita is written in which language?  
**A)** Hindi  
**B)** Pali  
**C)** Sanskrit  
**D)** Prakrit  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Bhagavad Gita is composed in Sanskrit.

### Question 9
**Q:** Gita's core message is about?  
**A)** Duty  
**B)** Devotion  
**C)** Knowledge  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Gita teaches duty (dharma), devotion (bhakti), and knowledge (jnana).

### Question 10
**Q:** First word of Bhagavad Gita is?  
**A)** Arjuna  
**B)** Dharmakshetra  
**C)** Krishna  
**D)** Pandava  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Gita begins with "Dharmakshetre Kurukshetre."

### Question 11
**Q:** Gita describes soul as?  
**A)** Mortal  
**B)** Immortal  
**C)** Changing  
**D)** Temporary  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Gita teaches that the soul (Atman) is eternal and immortal.

### Question 12
**Q:** Krishna showed his universal form to?  
**A)** Yudhishthira  
**B)** Arjuna  
**C)** Bhima  
**D)** Draupadi  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna revealed his Vishvarupa (universal form) to Arjuna in Chapter 11.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Total shlokas in Bhagavad Gita?  
**A)** 500  
**B)** 600  
**C)** 700  
**D)** 800  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Bhagavad Gita contains 700 verses (shlokas).

### Question 14
**Q:** Who narrated Gita to King Dhritarashtra?  
**A)** Vyasa  
**B)** Sanjaya  
**C)** Vidura  
**D)** Bhishma  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Sanjaya, gifted with divine vision, narrated the Gita to blind king Dhritarashtra.

### Question 15
**Q:** Which chapter describes various yogas?  
**A)** Chapter 2  
**B)** Chapter 6  
**C)** Chapter 12  
**D)** All chapters  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Different yogas are described throughout all 18 chapters.

### Question 16
**Q:** Krishna reveals he is?  
**A)** Human  
**B)** Avatar  
**C)** Supreme Being  
**D)** Demigod  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Krishna reveals himself as the Supreme Godhead (Purushottama).

### Question 17
**Q:** Gita's teaching on action is?  
**A)** Act without attachment  
**B)** Don't act  
**C)** Act for results  
**D)** Act selfishly  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Gita teaches Nishkama Karma - action without attachment to results.

### Question 18
**Q:** Which chapter is called Vishvarupa Darshana Yoga?  
**A)** Chapter 9  
**B)** Chapter 10  
**C)** Chapter 11  
**D)** Chapter 12  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Chapter 11 is Vishvarupa Darshana Yoga, describing the cosmic form.

### Question 19
**Q:** Gita describes three gunas as?  
**A)** Sattva, Rajas, Tamas  
**B)** Body, Mind, Soul  
**C)** Past, Present, Future  
**D)** Earth, Water, Fire  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The three gunas are Sattva (goodness), Rajas (passion), and Tamas (ignorance).

### Question 20
**Q:** Last chapter of Gita is about?  
**A)** War  
**B)** Moksha  
**C)** Renunciation  
**D)** Liberation  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Chapter 18 is Moksha Sannyasa Yoga - the yoga of liberation through renunciation.

### Question 21
**Q:** Arjuna addressed Krishna as?  
**A)** Friend  
**B)** Teacher  
**C)** God  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Arjuna called Krishna friend (Sakha), teacher (Guru), and God (Bhagavan).

### Question 22
**Q:** Gita's message is for?  
**A)** Warriors  
**B)** Sages  
**C)** All humans  
**D)** Hindus only  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Gita's teachings are universal and applicable to all humanity.

### Question 23
**Q:** Karma Yoga means?  
**A)** Yoga of action  
**B)** Yoga of knowledge  
**C)** Yoga of devotion  
**D)** Yoga of meditation  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Karma Yoga is the path of selfless action and duty.

### Question 24
**Q:** Which verse is most famous?  
**A)** 2.47  
**B)** 4.7  
**C)** 9.22  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Many verses like 2.47, 4.7-8, and 9.22 are extremely famous.

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25
**Q:** In which parva is Gita found?  
**A)** Shanti  
**B)** Bhishma  
**C)** Drona  
**D)** Udyoga  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Bhagavad Gita is in chapters 23-40 of the Bhishma Parva.

### Question 26
**Q:** Gita's philosophical school is?  
**A)** Advaita  
**B)** Dvaita  
**C)** Vishishtadvaita  
**D)** Syncretic  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Gita presents a syncretic philosophy incorporating multiple viewpoints.

### Question 27
**Q:** First English translation was in?  
**A)** 1785  
**B)** 1805  
**C)** 1825  
**D)** 1845  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Charles Wilkins published the first English translation in 1785.

### Question 28
**Q:** Which philosopher wrote Gita commentary?  
**A)** Shankaracharya  
**B)** Ramanuja  
**C)** Madhva  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** All three major Acharyas - Shankara, Ramanuja, and Madhva - wrote commentaries.

### Question 29
**Q:** Estimated period of Gita's composition?  
**A)** 3000 BCE  
**B)** 500 BCE  
**C)** 200 BCE  
**D)** 100 CE  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Scholars estimate the Gita was composed around 200 BCE to 200 CE.

### Question 30
**Q:** Which meter is used in Gita?  
**A)** Anushtup  
**B)** Trishtup  
**C)** Both Anushtup & Trishtup  
**D)** Gayatri  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Gita primarily uses Anushtup meter with some Trishtup verses.
