# Ramayana - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Smriti (Epics, Laws, Sutras)

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Who wrote Ramayana?  
**A)** Vyasa  
**B)** Valmiki  
**C)** Tulsidas  
**D)** Kalidasa  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sage Valmiki wrote the original Sanskrit Ramayana.

### Question 2
**Q:** Ramayana has how many kandas?  
**A)** 5  
**B)** 6  
**C)** 7  
**D)** 8  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ramayana has seven kandas (books): Bala, Ayodhya, Aranya, Kishkindha, Sundara, Yuddha, and Uttara.

### Question 3
**Q:** Rama's wife name is?  
**A)** Sita  
**B)** Draupadi  
**C)** Radha  
**D)** Rukmini  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sita is the wife of Lord Rama.

### Question 4
**Q:** Rama's father was?  
**A)** Janaka  
**B)** Dasharatha  
**C)** Kaikeya  
**D)** Bharata  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** King Dasharatha of Ayodhya was Rama's father.

### Question 5
**Q:** How many years was Rama exiled?  
**A)** 12  
**B)** 13  
**C)** 14  
**D)** 15  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rama was exiled for 14 years from Ayodhya.

### Question 6
**Q:** Who abducted Sita?  
**A)** Ravana  
**B)** Kumbhakarna  
**C)** Vibhishana  
**D)** Meghanada  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 7

**A)** Shiva  
**B)** Vayu  
**C)** Agni  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy

**D)** Kashi  
**Correct Answer:** C

**A)** 1

**D)** 4  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy

**A)** 5
