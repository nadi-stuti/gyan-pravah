## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Vishnu Purana is dedicated to?  
**A)** Shiva  
**B)** Vishnu  
**C)** Brahma  
**D)** Devi  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vishnu Purana is primarily dedicated to Lord Vishnu and his avatars.

### Question 2
**Q:** Vishnu Purana has how many books?  
**A)** 4  
**B)** 5  
**C)** 6  
**D)** 7  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vishnu Purana is divided into six books (amshas or parts).

### Question 3

**A)** 8  
**B)** 9  
**C)** 10  
**D)** 24

### Question 12
