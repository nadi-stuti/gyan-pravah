# Krishna (Bhagvan) - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Bhagvan (Deities)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Krishna was born in which city?  
**A)** Vrindavan  
**B)** Mathura  
**C)** Dwarka  
**D)** Gokul  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna was born in Mathura prison where his parents were imprisoned by Kamsa.

### Question 2
**Q:** Krishna's foster mother was?  
**A)** Devaki  
**B)** Yashoda  
**C)** Rohini  
**D)** Radha  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yashoda raised Krishna in Gokul after Vasudeva brought him there.

### Question 3
**Q:** Krishna is avatar of which deity?  
**A)** Shiva  
**B)** Brahma  
**C)** Vishnu  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna is considered the eighth avatar of Lord Vishnu.

### Question 4
**Q:** Krishna's favorite instrument was?  
**A)** Tabla  
**B)** Flute  
**C)** Veena  
**D)** Mridanga  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna is always depicted playing the flute (bansuri).

### Question 5
**Q:** Krishna lifted which mountain?  
**A)** Kailash  
**B)** Meru  
**C)** Govardhan  
**D)** Vindhya  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna lifted Govardhan mountain to protect villagers from Indra's wrath.

### Question 6
**Q:** Krishna's childhood friend was?  
**A)** Arjuna  
**B)** Sudama  
**C)** Bhima  
**D)** Karna  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sudama was Krishna's dear childhood friend and classmate.

### Question 7
**Q:** Krishna's consort in Dwarka was?  
**A)** Radha  
**B)** Rukmini  
**C)** Satyabhama  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rukmini was Krishna's principal queen in Dwarka.

### Question 8
**Q:** Krishna died due to?  
**A)** Old age  
**B)** War wound  
**C)** Hunter's arrow  
**D)** Poison  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** A hunter named Jara accidentally shot Krishna, mistaking him for a deer.

### Question 9
**Q:** Krishna's birth is celebrated as?  
**A)** Diwali  
**B)** Holi  
**C)** Janmashtami  
**D)** Dussehra  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Janmashtami celebrates Krishna's birth on the eighth day of Krishna Paksha.

### Question 10
**Q:** Krishna's complexion is described as?  
**A)** Golden  
**B)** Fair  
**C)** Dark blue  
**D)** Red  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna means "dark" and he's depicted with a dark blue complexion.

### Question 11
**Q:** Krishna's father was?  
**A)** Nanda  
**B)** Vasudeva  
**C)** Kamsa  
**D)** Ugrasena  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vasudeva was Krishna's biological father, though Nanda raised him.

### Question 12
**Q:** Krishna established which city?  
**A)** Mathura  
**B)** Vrindavan  
**C)** Dwarka  
**D)** Hastinapur  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna established the golden city of Dwarka in Gujarat.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** How many wives did Krishna have?  
**A)** 8  
**B)** 16,008  
**C)** 16,100  
**D)** 16,108  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Krishna had 16,108 wives including 8 principal queens and 16,100 rescued princesses.

### Question 14
**Q:** Krishna's guru was?  
**A)** Drona  
**B)** Sandipani  
**C)** Vasishtha  
**D)** Vishwamitra  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Sage Sandipani was Krishna and Balarama's teacher in Ujjain.

### Question 15
**Q:** Krishna killed Kamsa on which day?  
**A)** Diwali  
**B)** Holi  
**C)** Wrestling match  
**D)** Janmashtami  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Krishna killed his uncle Kamsa during a wrestling tournament in Mathura.

### Question 16
**Q:** Krishna's 8 principal queens are called?  
**A)** Ashtabharya  
**B)** Ashtapatni  
**C)** Ashtamahishi  
**D)** Ashtadevi  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The eight principal wives are collectively called Ashtabharya.

### Question 17
**Q:** Krishna's chariot flag had symbol of?  
**A)** Eagle  
**B)** Garuda  
**C)** Peacock  
**D)** Hanuman  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Krishna's chariot bore the Garuda flag (Garudadhvaja).

### Question 18
**Q:** Krishna's son who became king?  
**A)** Pradyumna  
**B)** Aniruddha  
**C)** Samba  
**D)** None  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Krishna didn't establish a dynasty; Dwarka submerged after his death.

### Question 19
**Q:** Krishna's famous dance is called?  
**A)** Tandava  
**B)** Rasa Lila  
**C)** Lasya  
**D)** Bharatanatyam  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rasa Lila is Krishna's divine dance with the gopis in Vrindavan.

### Question 20
**Q:** Krishna's conch shell was named?  
**A)** Panchajanya  
**B)** Devadatta  
**C)** Paundra  
**D)** Anantavijaya  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Krishna's conch was called Panchajanya, made from demon Panchajana's bones.

### Question 21
**Q:** Krishna appeared in which Yuga?  
**A)** Satya  
**B)** Treta  
**C)** Dwapara  
**D)** Kali  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Krishna incarnated at the end of Dwapara Yuga.

### Question 22
**Q:** Krishna's elder brother was?  
**A)** Balarama  
**B)** Gada  
**C)** Sarana  
**D)** Subhadra  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Balarama (Balabhadra) was Krishna's elder brother and constant companion.

### Question 23
**Q:** Krishna stole which item as child?  
**A)** Jewelry  
**B)** Butter  
**C)** Clothes  
**D)** Toys  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Krishna was famous for stealing butter (makhan) as a child, earning the name Makhan Chor.

### Question 24
**Q:** Krishna's favorite gopi was?  
**A)** Rukmini  
**B)** Radha  
**C)** Satyabhama  
**D)** Jambavati  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Radha was Krishna's beloved in Vrindavan, representing divine love.

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25
**Q:** Krishna lived for how many years?  
**A)** 125  
**B)** 126  
**C)** 127  
**D)** 128  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** According to Puranas, Krishna lived for 125 years and 8 months.

### Question 26
**Q:** Krishna's Sudarshana Chakra had how many spokes?  
**A)** 6  
**B)** 8  
**C)** 10  
**D)** 12  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Sudarshana Chakra is described as having six spokes in ancient texts.

### Question 27
**Q:** Krishna's birth star (nakshatra) was?  
**A)** Rohini  
**B)** Pushya  
**C)** Ashlesha  
**D)** Magha  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Krishna was born under Rohini nakshatra on Ashtami tithi.

### Question 28
**Q:** Krishna's Dwarka had how many gates?  
**A)** 4  
**B)** 6  
**C)** 8  
**D)** 12  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The golden city of Dwarka had twelve magnificent gates.

### Question 29
**Q:** Krishna's Yadava clan belonged to which varna?  
**A)** Brahmin  
**B)** Kshatriya  
**C)** Vaishya  
**D)** Mixed  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Yadavas were a Kshatriya (warrior) clan, though they were also cowherds.

### Question 30
**Q:** Krishna's departure from earth is called?  
**A)** Moksha  
**B)** Nirvana  
**C)** Niryaana  
**D)** Videha Mukti  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Krishna's departure from his physical form is termed as Niryaana in scriptures.
