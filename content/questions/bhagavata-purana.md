# Bhagavata Purana - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Purana (Mythology & Theology)  
**Total Questions:** 30

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Bhagavata Purana mainly describes?  
**A)** Shiva  
**B)** Krishna  
**C)** Rama  
**D)** Brahma  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Bhagavata Purana primarily describes the life and teachings of Lord Krishna.

### Question 2
**Q:** How many skandhas in Bhagavata?  
**A)** 10  
**B)** 11  
**C)** 12  
**D)** 13  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Bhagavata Purana is divided into 12 skandhas (cantos).

### Question 3
**Q:** Who narrated Bhagavata to Parikshit?  
**A)** Vyasa  
**B)** Shuka  
**C)** Suta  
**D)** Narada  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sage Shuka narrated Bhagavata Purana to King Parikshit.

### Question 4
**Q:** Bhagavata Purana is written in?  
**A)** Hindi  
**B)** Prakrit  
**C)** Sanskrit  
**D)** Pali  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Bhagavata Purana is composed in Sanskrit language.

### Question 5
**Q:** King Parikshit had how many days to live?  
**A)** 3  
**B)** 5  
**C)** 7  
**D)** 9  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Parikshit was cursed to die in seven days from a serpent bite.

### Question 6
**Q:** Bhagavata emphasizes which yoga?  
**A)** Karma  
**B)** Jnana  
**C)** Bhakti  
**D)** Raja  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Bhagavata Purana primarily emphasizes Bhakti Yoga (devotion).

### Question 7
**Q:** Which skandha describes Krishna's childhood?  
**A)** 8th  
**B)** 9th  
**C)** 10th  
**D)** 11th  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The 10th Canto extensively describes Krishna's childhood and youth.

### Question 8
**Q:** Bhagavata is one of the?  
**A)** 12 Puranas  
**B)** 18 Puranas  
**C)** 24 Puranas  
**D)** 36 Puranas  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 9

**A)** Valmiki  
**B)** Vyasa  
**C)** Suta  
**D)** Shuka  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 10
**Q:** Bhagavata describes how many avatars?  
**A)** All 10  
**B)** 22  
**C)** 24  
**D)** Countless  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 11
**Q:** Rasa Lila is described in which skandha?  
**A)** 8th  
**B)** 9th  
**C)** 10th  
**D)** 11th  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 12
**Q:** Bhagavata teaches primarily about?  
**A)** Dharma  
**B)** Bhakti  
**C)** Moksha  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Bhagavata teaches dharma, bhakti, and the path to moksha.
