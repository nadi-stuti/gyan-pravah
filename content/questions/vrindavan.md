## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Vrindavan is associated with which deity?  
**A)** Rama  
**B)** Krishna  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Ganesha  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vrindavan is the holy land where Lord Krishna spent his childhood.

### Question 2
**Q:** Vrindavan is located in which state?  
**A)** Bihar  
**B)** Rajasthan  
**C)** Uttar Pradesh  
**D)** Madhya Pradesh  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vrindavan is in the Mathura district of Uttar Pradesh.

### Question 3
**Q:** Which river flows near Vrindavan?  
**A)** Ganga  
**B)** Yamuna  
**C)** Saraswati  
**D)** Narmada  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The holy Yamuna river flows through Vrindavan.

### Question 4
