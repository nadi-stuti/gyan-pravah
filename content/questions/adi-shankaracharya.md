## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Adi Shankaracharya was born in?  
**A)** Tamil Nadu  
**B)** Kerala  
**C)** Karnataka  
**D)** Andhra Pradesh  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Adi Shankaracharya was born in Kaladi village in Kerala.

### Question 2
**Q:** Shankaracharya's philosophy is called?  
**A)** Dvaita  
**B)** Advaita  
**C)** Vishishtadvaita  
**D)** Dvaitadvaita  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Shankaracharya established Advaita Vedanta philosophy of non-dualism.

### Question 3
**Q:** Shankaracharya lived in which century?  
**A)** 6th-7th CE  
**B)** 7th-8th CE  
**C)** 8th-9th CE  
**D)** 9th-10th CE  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Adi Shankaracharya lived in the 8th-9th century CE (788-820 CE traditionally).

### Question 4
**Q:** Shankaracharya established how many main mathas?  
**A)** 2  
**B)** 3  
**C)** 4  
**D)** 5  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** He established four main mathas (monasteries) in four directions of India.

### Question 5

**Explanation:** Advaita literally means "not two," indicating non-dualistic reality.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Shankaracharya's four mathas are located in?  
**A)** Sringeri, Dwarka, Puri, Joshimath  
**B)** Kanchipuram, Sringeri, Puri, Dwarka  
**C)** Badrinath, Puri, Dwarka, Sringeri  
**D)** Various locations  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The four cardinal mathas are at Sringeri (South), Dwarka (West), Puri (East), and Joshimath (North).

### Question 14

**A)** Karma vs. Jnana  
**B)** Ritual vs. Knowledge  
**C)** Householder vs. Renunciant life  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The famous debate covered the relative importance of ritual action versus spiritual knowledge.

### Question 15

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25

**B)** 788-820 CE vs. earlier dates  
**C)** Multiple historical references  
**D)** All options

**Difficulty:** Hard

### Question 26
**Q:** Shankaracharya's philosophical methodology employed?  
**A)** Adhyaropa-apavada  
**B)** Superimposition-negation  
**C)** Progressive teaching  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** His pedagogical method used superimposition and subsequent negation to lead students to truth.

### Question 27
**Q:** Shankaracharya's critique of other schools included?  
**A)** Buddhist Vijnanavada  
**B)** Samkhya dualism  
**C)** Mimamsa ritualism  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** He systematically refuted various philosophical schools while establishing Advaita supremacy.

### Question 28
**Q:** Shankaracharya's concept of "Adhyasa" means?  
**A)** Superimposition  
**B)** Mistaken identity  
**C)** Fundamental error  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Adhyasa is the fundamental error of superimposing the unreal on the real, causing bondage.

### Question 29
**Q:** Shankaracharya's institutional legacy includes?  
**A)** Continuous succession  
**B)** Preserved teachings  
**C)** Living tradition  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** His institutional framework has maintained an unbroken tradition for over 1200 years.

### Question 30
**Q:** Shankaracharya's ultimate teaching is?  
**A)** Tat tvam asi realization  
**B)** Atman-Brahman identity  
**C)** Liberation through knowledge  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** His core teaching is the direct realization of one's true nature as Brahman, leading to liberation.
