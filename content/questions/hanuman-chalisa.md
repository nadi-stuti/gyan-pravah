# Hanuman Chalisa - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Stuti (Hymns & Prayers)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Who wrote Hanuman Chalisa?  
**A)** Valmiki  
**B)** Tulsidas  
**C)** Surdas  
**D)** Kabir  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sant Tulsidas composed the Hanuman Chalisa.

### Question 2
**Q:** How many verses in Hanuman Chalisa?  
**A)** 40  
**B)** 42  
**C)** 44  
**D)** 50  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman Chalisa contains 40 verses (chaupais), hence the name "Chalisa."

### Question 3
**Q:** Hanuman Chalisa is written in which language?  
**A)** Sanskrit  
**B)** Hindi  
**C)** Awadhi  
**D)** Braj  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman Chalisa is written in Awadhi language.

### Question 4
**Q:** Hanuman is son of which god?  
**A)** Shiva  
**B)** Vayu  
**C)** Agni  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman is the son of Vayu (wind god).

### Question 5
**Q:** Hanuman is devotee of?  
**A)** Krishna  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Rama  
**D)** Vishnu  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman is the greatest devotee of Lord Rama.

### Question 6
**Q:** Hanuman is also known as?  
**A)** Bajrangbali  
**B)** Maruti  
**C)** Anjaneya  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman is known by many names: Bajrangbali, Maruti, and Anjaneya.

### Question 7
**Q:** Hanuman Chalisa begins with praise to?  
**A)** Rama  
**B)** Guru  
**C)** Hanuman  
**D)** Both Rama & Guru  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman Chalisa begins with "Shri Guru Charan Saroj Raj," praising the guru.

### Question 8
**Q:** Hanuman carried which mountain?  
**A)** Kailash  
**B)** Meru  
**C)** Dronagiri  
**D)** Govardhan  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman carried the Dronagiri mountain to save Lakshmana.

### Question 9
**Q:** Hanuman is considered avatar of?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Brahma  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman is considered an avatar of Lord Shiva.

### Question 10
**Q:** What is the benefit of reciting Hanuman Chalisa?  
**A)** Strength  
**B)** Protection  
**C)** Wisdom  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Reciting Hanuman Chalisa gives strength, protection, and wisdom.

### Question 11
**Q:** Hanuman is depicted carrying?  
**A)** Gada (mace)  
**B)** Mountain  
**C)** Both Gada (mace) & Mountain  
**D)** Bow  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman is shown carrying a gada and sometimes the Sanjeevani mountain.

### Question 12
**Q:** Hanuman found Sita in?  
**A)** Ayodhya  
**B)** Lanka  
**C)** Kishkindha  
**D)** Mithila  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman found Sita in Lanka in Ashoka Vatika.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** In which century was Hanuman Chalisa composed?  
**A)** 14th  
**B)** 15th  
**C)** 16th  
**D)** 17th  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Tulsidas composed Hanuman Chalisa in the 16th century (around 1590s).

### Question 14
**Q:** Hanuman Chalisa has how many dohas?  
**A)** 2  
**B)** 3  
**C)** 4  
**D)** 5  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Hanuman Chalisa has 2 dohas (couplets) at the beginning and end.

### Question 15
**Q:** According to Chalisa, what size did Hanuman assume?  
**A)** Large as mountain  
**B)** Small as fly  
**C)** Both Large as mountain & Small as fly  
**D)** Normal  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Hanuman could assume any size - as large as a mountain or as small as a fly.

### Question 16

**C)** Ashwagandha  
**D)** Brahmi

**Difficulty:** Medium

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25
**Q:** Total words in Hanuman Chalisa approximately?  
**A)** 250  
**B)** 350  
**C)** 450  
**D)** 550  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Hanuman Chalisa contains approximately 450 words in total.

### Question 26
**Q:** According to Chalisa, distance Hanuman jumped?  
**A)** 50 yojanas  
**B)** 100 yojanas  
**C)** 150 yojanas  
**D)** 200 yojanas  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Chalisa mentions Hanuman jumped 100 yojanas (about 800 miles) to reach Lanka.

### Question 27
**Q:** Which meter is Hanuman Chalisa composed in?  
**A)** Doha  
**B)** Chaupai  
**C)** Soratha  
**D)** Mix  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Hanuman Chalisa uses a mix of Chaupai and Doha meters.

### Question 28
**Q:** Hanuman is considered Chiranjeevi (immortal) because?  
**A)** Rama's blessing  
**B)** Brahma's boon  
**C)** His devotion  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Hanuman's immortality is due to blessings from Rama, Brahma, and his supreme devotion.

### Question 29
**Q:** The last line of Hanuman Chalisa invokes?  
**A)** Hanuman  
**B)** Rama-Lakshman  
**C)** Sita  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The final doha invokes blessings of Hanuman, Rama, Lakshman, and Sita.

### Question 30
**Q:** Tulsidas wrote Hanuman Chalisa in which place?  
**A)** Varanasi  
**B)** Ayodhya  
**C)** Chitrakoot  
**D)** Mathura  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Tulsidas composed Hanuman Chalisa in Varanasi.
