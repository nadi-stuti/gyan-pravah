# Rama - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Bhagvan (Deities)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Rama was prince of which kingdom?  
**A)** Hastinapur  
**B)** Ayodhya  
**C)** Mathura  
**D)** Lanka  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rama was the prince of Ayodhya, ruled by his father Dasharatha.

### Question 2
**Q:** Rama's wife was?  
**A)** Draupadi  
**B)** Sita  
**C)** Radha  
**D)** Rukmini  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sita was Rama's devoted wife, daughter of King Janaka.

### Question 3
**Q:** Rama's loyal devotee was?  
**A)** Garuda  
**B)** Nandi  
**C)** Hanuman  
**D)** Shesha  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Hanuman was Rama's greatest devotee and helped rescue Sita.

### Question 4
**Q:** Rama was exiled for how many years?  
**A)** 12  
**B)** 13  
**C)** 14  
**D)** 15  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rama was exiled to the forest for fourteen years.

### Question 5
**Q:** Rama killed which demon king?  
**A)** Hiranyakashipu  
**B)** Ravana  
**C)** Mahishasura  
**D)** Kamsa  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rama killed the ten-headed demon king Ravana of Lanka.

### Question 6
**Q:** Rama's younger brother was?  
**A)** Bharata  
**B)** Lakshmana  
**C)** Shatrughna  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rama had three younger brothers: Lakshmana, Bharata, and Shatrughna.

### Question 7
**Q:** Rama's story is told in?  
**A)** Mahabharata  
**B)** Ramayana  
**C)** Puranas  
**D)** Vedas  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Ramayana, composed by Valmiki, narrates Rama's life story.

### Question 8
**Q:** Rama is avatar of?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Brahma  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rama is considered the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu.

### Question 9
**Q:** Rama's father was?  
**A)** Janaka  
**B)** Dasharatha  
**C)** Raghu  
**D)** Aja  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** King Dasharatha of Ayodhya was Rama's father.

### Question 10
**Q:** Rama's return is celebrated as?  
**A)** Holi  
**B)** Diwali  
**C)** Dussehra  
**D)** Both B & C  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Dussehra celebrates Ravana's defeat, Diwali celebrates Rama's return to Ayodhya.

### Question 11
**Q:** Rama broke whose bow?  
**A)** Ravana's  
**B)** Shiva's  
**C)** Indra's  
**D)** Arjuna's  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rama broke Shiva's bow in Sita's swayamvara to win her hand.

### Question 12
**Q:** Rama's birth is celebrated as?  
**A)** Ram Navami  
**B)** Janmashtami  
**C)** Mahashivratri  
**D)** Hanuman Jayanti  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ram Navami celebrates Rama's birth on the ninth day of Chaitra month.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Rama was born in which Treta Yuga cycle?  
**A)** 24th  
**B)** 25th  
**C)** 26th  
**D)** 27th  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rama incarnated in the 24th Treta Yuga of the current Kalpa.

### Question 14
**Q:** Rama's guru was?  
**A)** Drona  
**B)** Vishwamitra  
**C)** Vasishtha  
**D)** Both B & C  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Vasishtha was family guru, Vishwamitra taught him warfare and mantras.

### Question 15
**Q:** Rama's dynasty was called?  
**A)** Chandravamsha  
**B)** Suryavamsha  
**C)** Agnivamsha  
**D)** Nagavamsha  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rama belonged to the Suryavamsha (Solar dynasty) of Ikshvaku.

### Question 16
**Q:** Rama ruled Ayodhya for how many years?  
**A)** 10,000  
**B)** 11,000  
**C)** 12,000  
**D)** 13,000  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rama ruled for 11,000 years during Ram Rajya (ideal governance).

### Question 17
**Q:** Rama's sons were?  
**A)** Luv and Kush  
**B)** Angad and Sugriva  
**C)** Bharata and Shatrughna  
**D)** Hanuman and Jambavan  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Luv and Kush were Rama's twin sons born to Sita in exile.

### Question 18
**Q:** Rama's weapon of choice was?  
**A)** Sword  
**B)** Mace  
**C)** Bow and arrow  
**D)** Trident  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rama was an expert archer, famous for his bow Kodanda.

### Question 19
**Q:** Rama's mother was?  
**A)** Kaushalya  
**B)** Kaikeyi  
**C)** Sumitra  
**D)** Mandodari  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Queen Kaushalya was Rama's mother, Dasharatha's first wife.

### Question 20
**Q:** Rama met Hanuman through?  
**A)** Sugriva  
**B)** Jambavan  
**C)** Angad  
**D)** Vibhishana  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Hanuman first met Rama while serving Sugriva, the monkey king.

### Question 21
**Q:** Rama's famous bow was called?  
**A)** Gandiva  
**B)** Kodanda  
**C)** Vijaya  
**D)** Sharanga  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rama's divine bow was called Kodanda, given by sage Agastya.

### Question 22
**Q:** Rama crossed ocean with help of?  
**A)** Garuda  
**B)** Hanuman  
**C)** Nala-Nila  
**D)** Varuna  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Nala and Nila built the bridge (Rama Setu) across the ocean to Lanka.

### Question 23
**Q:** Rama's ideal is called?  
**A)** Ram Rajya  
**B)** Maryada Purushottam  
**C)** Dharma Raja  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rama embodies all these ideals - perfect governance, righteousness, and dharma.

### Question 24
**Q:** Rama performed which sacrifice?  
**A)** Rajasuya  
**B)** Ashwamedha  
**C)** Vajapeya  
**D)** Agnihotra  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rama performed the Ashwamedha sacrifice to establish his sovereignty.

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25
**Q:** Rama's birth nakshatra was?  
**A)** Punarvasu  
**B)** Pushya  
**C)** Ashlesha  
**D)** Magha  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Rama was born under Punarvasu nakshatra in Chaitra month.

### Question 26
**Q:** Rama's departure from earth is called?  
**A)** Jal Samadhi  
**B)** Videha Mukti  
**C)** Saryu Pravesh  
**D)** Moksha  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Rama took Jal Samadhi (water burial) in river Saryu at Ayodhya.

### Question 27
**Q:** Rama's genealogy traces back to?  
**A)** Brahma  
**B)** Surya  
**C)** Ikshvaku  
**D)** Manu  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Rama's lineage traces to Ikshvaku, son of Manu, founder of Solar dynasty.

### Question 28
**Q:** Rama lived for how many years?  
**A)** 11,000  
**B)** 11,100  
**C)** 11,200  
**D)** 11,300  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Rama lived for approximately 11,000 years according to Puranic calculations.

### Question 29
**Q:** Rama's coronation day is called?  
**A)** Ram Rajyabhishek  
**B)** Deepavali  
**C)** Both A & B  
**D)** Ram Navami  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Rama's coronation (Rajyabhishek) is celebrated as the original Deepavali.

### Question 30
**Q:** Rama's famous mantra is?  
**A)** Om Ram Ramaya Namah  
**B)** Sri Ram Jai Ram  
**C)** Ram Nam Satya Hai  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** All these are popular mantras dedicated to Lord Rama in different traditions.
