# Rigveda - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Shruti (Vedic Scriptures)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Rigveda has how many mandalas?  
**A)** 8  
**B)** 9  
**C)** 10  
**D)** 12  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Rigveda is divided into 10 mandalas (books or circles).

### Question 2
**Q:** Rigveda is primarily composed of?  
**A)** Stories  
**B)** Hymns  
**C)** Laws  
**D)** Rituals  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rigveda consists mainly of hymns (suktas) praising various deities.

### Question 3
**Q:** Gayatri Mantra is from which Veda?  
**A)** Rigveda  
**B)** Samaveda  
**C)** Yajurveda  
**D)** Atharvaveda  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The famous Gayatri Mantra is found in Rigveda (3.62.10).

### Question 4
**Q:** Rigveda is written in which language?  
**A)** Sanskrit  
**B)** Vedic Sanskrit  
**C)** Pali  
**D)** Prakrit  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rigveda is composed in Vedic Sanskrit, the oldest form of Sanskrit.

### Question 5
**Q:** Rigveda hymns are called?  
**A)** Suktas  
**B)** Shlokas  
**C)** Mantras  
**D)** Richas  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Individual hymns in Rigveda are called suktas (well-spoken).

### Question 6
**Q:** Rigveda is oldest of?  
**A)** Puranas  
**B)** Vedas  
**C)** Upanishads  
**D)** Epics  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rigveda is considered the oldest of the four Vedas.

### Question 7
**Q:** Main deity praised in Rigveda is?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Indra  
**D)** Brahma  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Indra, the king of gods, is the most frequently mentioned deity in Rigveda.

### Question 8
**Q:** Rigveda verses are called?  
**A)** Richas  
**B)** Yajus  
**C)** Saman  
**D)** Atharvan  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Individual verses of Rigveda are called richas or riks.

### Question 9
**Q:** Rigveda belongs to which category?  
**A)** Shruti  
**B)** Smriti  
**C)** Purana  
**D)** Itihasa  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rigveda is part of Shruti literature, considered divinely revealed.

### Question 10
**Q:** Rigveda was preserved through?  
**A)** Writing  
**B)** Oral tradition  
**C)** Stone inscriptions  
**D)** Palm leaves  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rigveda was preserved for millennia through oral tradition and memorization.

### Question 11
**Q:** Rigveda mentions which river most?  
**A)** Ganga  
**B)** Yamuna  
**C)** Saraswati  
**D)** Sindhu  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** River Sindhu (Indus) is mentioned most frequently in Rigveda.

### Question 12
**Q:** Rigveda's geographical setting is?  
**A)** Gangetic plains  
**B)** Deccan plateau  
**C)** Northwestern India  
**D)** Southern India  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rigveda's geographical references point to northwestern India and Afghanistan.
