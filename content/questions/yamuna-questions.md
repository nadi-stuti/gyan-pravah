# Yamuna - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Nadi (Holy Rivers)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Yamuna is associated with which deity?  
**A)** Rama  
**B)** Krishna  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Ganesha  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yamuna is closely associated with Lord Krishna who performed many leelas on her banks.

### Question 2
**Q:** Yamuna originates from which place?  
**A)** Kailash  
**B)** Govardhan  
**C)** Yamunotri  
**D)** Vindhya  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yamuna originates from the Yamunotri glacier in Uttarakhand.

### Question 3
**Q:** Krishna played on banks of?  
**A)** Ganga  
**B)** Yamuna  
**C)** Saraswati  
**D)** Narmada  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna's childhood leelas took place on the banks of Yamuna in Vrindavan.

### Question 4
**Q:** Yamuna meets Ganga at?  
**A)** Haridwar  
**B)** Prayagraj  
**C)** Varanasi  
**D)** Kanpur  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yamuna meets Ganga at Prayagraj (Allahabad), forming the sacred Sangam.

### Question 5
**Q:** Yamuna flows through which capital city?  
**A)** Mumbai  
**B)** Kolkata  
**C)** Delhi  
**D)** Chennai  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yamuna flows through Delhi, the national capital of India.

### Question 6
**Q:** Which city is on Yamuna's bank in UP?  
**A)** Lucknow  
**B)** Mathura  
**C)** Varanasi  
**D)** Kanpur  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Mathura, Krishna's birthplace, is situated on the banks of Yamuna.

### Question 7
**Q:** Yamuna is sister of?  
**A)** Surya  
**B)** Yama  
**C)** Ganga  
**D)** Krishna  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yamuna is the twin sister of Yama, the god of death.

### Question 8
**Q:** Which famous monument is on Yamuna's bank?  
**A)** Red Fort  
**B)** Taj Mahal  
**C)** Qutub Minar  
**D)** India Gate  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The iconic Taj Mahal in Agra is situated on the banks of river Yamuna.

### Question 9
**Q:** Yamuna is considered daughter of?  
**A)** Shiva  
**B)** Vishnu  
**C)** Surya  
**D)** Brahma  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yamuna is considered the daughter of Surya (Sun god).

### Question 10
**Q:** The serpent Kaliya lived in?  
**A)** Ganga  
**B)** Yamuna  
**C)** Narmada  
**D)** Godavari  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The serpent Kaliya lived in Yamuna until Krishna subdued him.

### Question 11
**Q:** Yamuna is also known as?  
**A)** Bhagirathi  
**B)** Kalindi  
**C)** Mandakini  
**D)** Alakananda  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yamuna is also known as Kalindi, one of Krishna's eight principal queens.

### Question 12
**Q:** Which state does Yamuna NOT flow through?  
**A)** Uttarakhand  
**B)** Haryana  
**C)** Bihar  
**D)** UP  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yamuna flows through Uttarakhand, Haryana, Delhi, and UP but not Bihar.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Yamuna's water appears which color?  
**A)** Clear  
**B)** Blue  
**C)** Dark  
**D)** Green  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Yamuna's water often appears dark, especially near Delhi due to pollution.

### Question 14
**Q:** How many major tributaries does Yamuna have?  
**A)** Five  
**B)** Seven  
**C)** Nine  
**D)** Eleven  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Yamuna has seven major tributaries including Chambal, Betwa, and Ken.

### Question 15
**Q:** Which is the longest tributary of Yamuna?  
**A)** Chambal  
**B)** Betwa  
**C)** Ken  
**D)** Tons  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Chambal is the longest tributary of Yamuna at about 960 km.

### Question 16
**Q:** Yamuna originates from which mountain range?  
**A)** Aravallis  
**B)** Vindhyas  
**C)** Himalayas  
**D)** Western Ghats  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Yamuna originates from the Bandarpunch peaks in the Himalayas.

### Question 17
**Q:** Which Mughal garden is on Yamuna's bank?  
**A)** Shalimar Bagh  
**B)** Nishat Bagh  
**C)** Mehtab Bagh  
**D)** Lodhi Garden  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Mehtab Bagh in Agra, facing the Taj Mahal, is on Yamuna's bank.

### Question 18
**Q:** Yamuna Action Plan was launched in?  
**A)** 1990  
**B)** 1993  
**C)** 1996  
**D)** 1999  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Yamuna Action Plan was launched in 1993 to clean the river.

### Question 19
**Q:** Which barrage is on river Yamuna?  
**A)** Farakka  
**B)** Hathni Kund  
**C)** Okhla  
**D)** Both B & C  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Both Hathni Kund Barrage and Okhla Barrage are built on Yamuna.

### Question 20
**Q:** Yamuna basin covers which states primarily?  
**A)** UP, Haryana  
**B)** MP, Rajasthan  
**C)** Delhi, Uttarakhand  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Yamuna basin covers UP, Haryana, MP, Rajasthan, Delhi, and Uttarakhand.

### Question 21
**Q:** Which ancient city was on Yamuna?  
**A)** Indraprastha  
**B)** Hastinapur  
**C)** Both A & B  
**D)** Neither  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Both Indraprastha (ancient Delhi) and Hastinapur were on Yamuna's banks.

### Question 22
**Q:** Yamuna contributes what percentage to Ganga's flow?  
**A)** 30%  
**B)** 40%  
**C)** 50%  
**D)** 60%  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Yamuna contributes approximately 60% of Ganga's flow at their confluence.

### Question 23
**Q:** Which festival is celebrated on Yamuna's banks?  
**A)** Yamuna Chhath  
**B)** Kumbh Mela  
**C)** Yamuna Jayanti  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** All these festivals—Yamuna Chhath, Kumbh Mela, and Yamuna Jayanti—are celebrated on Yamuna.

### Question 24
**Q:** The Wazirabad barrage supplies water to?  
**A)** Delhi  
**B)** Agra  
**C)** Mathura  
**D)** Vrindavan  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Wazirabad barrage on Yamuna is a major water source for Delhi.
