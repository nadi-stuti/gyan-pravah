## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Who wrote Ramcharitmanas?  
**A)** Valmiki  
**B)** Tulsidas  
**C)** Surdas  
**D)** Kabir  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Goswami Tulsidas wrote the Ramcharitmanas.

### Question 2
**Q:** In which language did Tulsidas write?  
**A)** Sanskrit  
**B)** Hindi  
**C)** Awadhi  
**D)** Braj  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Tulsidas wrote Ramcharitmanas in Awadhi language.

### Question 3
**Q:** Tulsidas lived in which century?  
**A)** 14th  
**B)** 15th  
**C)** 16th  
**D)** 17th  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Tulsidas lived in the 16th century (1532-1623).

### Question 4
**Q:** Tulsidas was devotee of?  
**A)** Krishna  
**B)** Rama  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Hanuman  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Tulsidas was a devoted follower of Lord Rama.

### Question 5

**A)** Valmiki's Ramayana  
**B)** Mahabharata  
**C)** Puranas  
**D)** Vedas  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 6

**B)** Varanasi

**Difficulty:** Easy

**A)** Goswami
