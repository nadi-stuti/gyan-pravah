## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** How many names in Vishnu Sahasranama?  
**A)** 100  
**B)** 500  
**C)** 1000  
**D)** 1008  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vishnu Sahasranama contains 1000 names of Lord Vishnu.

### Question 2
**Q:** Who taught Vishnu Sahasranama to Yudhishthira?  
**A)** Vyasa  
**B)** Bhishma  
**C)** Krishna  
**D)** Narada  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Bhishma taught Vishnu Sahasranama to Yudhishthira on his deathbed.

### Question 3

**A)** Ramayana  
**B)** Mahabharata  
**C)** Bhagavata

**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vishnu Sahasranama is part of the Mahabharata's Anushasana Parva.
