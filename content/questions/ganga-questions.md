# Ganga - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Nadi (Holy Rivers)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Where does Ganga originate?  
**A)** Gangotri  
**B)** Haridwar  
**C)** Rishikesh  
**D)** Kedarnath  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga originates from the Gangotri glacier in Uttarakhand.

### Question 2
**Q:** Ganga flows through which plains?  
**A)** Deccan  
**B)** Indo-Gangetic  
**C)** Malwa  
**D)** Thar  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga flows through the Indo-Gangetic plains, one of the most fertile regions.

### Question 3
**Q:** Ganga is considered daughter of?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Brahma  
**D)** Himavan  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga is considered the daughter of King Himavan (the Himalayas personified).

### Question 4
**Q:** Which city is famous for Ganga Aarti?  
**A)** Haridwar  
**B)** Varanasi  
**C)** Both Haridwar & Varanasi  
**D)** Rishikesh  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Both Haridwar and Varanasi are famous for their daily Ganga Aarti ceremonies.

### Question 5
**Q:** Ganga finally merges into which body of water?  
**A)** Arabian Sea  
**B)** Bay of Bengal  
**C)** Indian Ocean  
**D)** Andaman Sea  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga flows eastward and merges into the Bay of Bengal.

### Question 6
**Q:** Which Hindu god is closely associated with Ganga?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Brahma  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Lord Shiva caught Ganga in his matted locks to control her descent to earth.

### Question 7
**Q:** Ganga is also known as?  
**A)** Kalindi  
**B)** Bhagirathi  
**C)** Alakananda  
**D)** Mandakini  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga is also called Bhagirathi after King Bhagiratha who brought her to earth.

### Question 8
**Q:** Which states does Ganga flow through?  
**A)** UP, Bihar  
**B)** UK, WB  
**C)** Jharkhand  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga flows through Uttarakhand, UP, Bihar, Jharkhand, and West Bengal.

### Question 9
**Q:** Ganga water is considered?  
**A)** Holy  
**B)** Purifying  
**C)** Sacred  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga water is considered holy, purifying, and sacred in Hindu tradition.

### Question 10
**Q:** Which festival involves bathing in Ganga?  
**A)** Kumbh Mela  
**B)** Diwali  
**C)** Holi  
**D)** Dussehra  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Kumbh Mela involves ritual bathing in sacred rivers including Ganga.

### Question 11
**Q:** The Ganga is mentioned in which ancient texts?  
**A)** Vedas  
**B)** Puranas  
**C)** Epics  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga is mentioned extensively in Vedas, Puranas, and epics like Ramayana and Mahabharata.

### Question 12
**Q:** What is the symbol of Ganga?  
**A)** Swan  
**B)** Crocodile  
**C)** Dolphin  
**D)** Fish  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga's vahana (vehicle) is the Makara, often depicted as a crocodile.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** How many major tributaries does Ganga have?  
**A)** Seven  
**B)** Nine  
**C)** Eleven  
**D)** Thirteen  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganga has eleven major tributaries including Yamuna, Gandak, and Kosi.

### Question 14
**Q:** King Bhagiratha brought Ganga for?  
**A)** Agriculture  
**B)** Salvation of ancestors  
**C)** Power  
**D)** Beauty  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Bhagiratha brought Ganga to provide salvation to his 60,000 ancestors.

### Question 15
**Q:** Which tributary meets Ganga at Prayagraj?  
**A)** Yamuna  
**B)** Gomti  
**C)** Son  
**D)** Gandak  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Yamuna meets Ganga at Prayagraj (Allahabad), forming the Sangam.

### Question 16
**Q:** Ganga descends from heaven in which form?  
**A)** Gentle stream  
**B)** Torrential force  
**C)** Rain  
**D)** Mist  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganga descended as a torrential force, which Shiva controlled in his locks.

### Question 17
**Q:** Which national park protects Ganga dolphins?  
**A)** Kaziranga  
**B)** Vikramshila  
**C)** Jim Corbett  
**D)** Sundarbans  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary in Bihar protects these endangered dolphins.

### Question 18
**Q:** The Ganga Action Plan was launched in?  
**A)** 1980  
**B)** 1985  
**C)** 1990  
**D)** 1995  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Ganga Action Plan was launched in 1985 to clean the river.

### Question 19
**Q:** Which hydroelectric project is on Ganga?  
**A)** Tehri Dam  
**B)** Bhakra Dam  
**C)** Sardar Sarovar  
**D)** Hirakud Dam  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Tehri Dam on the Bhagirathi River (Ganga tributary) is one of the highest dams in India.

### Question 20
**Q:** Ganga is declared National River in?  
**A)** 2005  
**B)** 2008  
**C)** 2010  
**D)** 2012  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganga was declared India's National River in November 2008.

### Question 21
**Q:** Which ancient city was built on Ganga banks?  
**A)** Pataliputra  
**B)** Hastinapur  
**C)** Kashi  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** All these ancient cities—Pataliputra, Hastinapur, and Kashi—were built on Ganga's banks.

### Question 22
**Q:** The Ganga basin covers which percentage of India?  
**A)** 16%  
**B)** 26%  
**C)** 36%  
**D)** 46%  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Ganga basin covers about 26% of India's landmass.

### Question 23
**Q:** Which goddess form is Ganga associated with?  
**A)** Lakshmi  
**B)** Saraswati  
**C)** Parvati  
**D)** Ganga Devi  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganga is worshipped as Ganga Devi or Goddess Ganga.

### Question 24
**Q:** What is the main source stream of Ganga?  
**A)** Alakananda  
**B)** Bhagirathi  
**C)** Mandakini  
**D)** Pindar  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Bhagirathi, originating from Gangotri, is considered the main source stream.

**Difficulty:** Hard

### Question 28
**Q:** Ganga originates at what altitude?

**D)** 5,000 m

**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Gangotri glacier, source of Ganga, is at an altitude of 3,892 meters.
