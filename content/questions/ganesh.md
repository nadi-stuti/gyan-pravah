# Ganesh - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Bhagvan (Deities)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Ganesh is son of which deity?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Brahma  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Lord Ganesh is the son of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati.

### Question 2
**Q:** Ganesh has head of which animal?  
**A)** Lion  
**B)** Tiger  
**C)** Elephant  
**D)** Horse  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesh has the head of an elephant, making him easily recognizable.

### Question 3
**Q:** Ganesh is remover of?  
**A)** Poverty  
**B)** Obstacles  
**C)** Diseases  
**D)** Enemies  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesh is known as Vighnaharta - the remover of obstacles.

### Question 4
**Q:** Ganesh's vehicle is?  
**A)** Lion  
**B)** Mouse  
**C)** Peacock  
**D)** Bull  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesh rides on a mouse (Mushika), symbolizing control over desires.

### Question 5
**Q:** Ganesh is worshipped before?  
**A)** Any new venture  
**B)** Meals  
**C)** Sleep  
**D)** Travel  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesh is invoked at the beginning of any new work or venture.

### Question 6
**Q:** Ganesh's mother is?  
**A)** Lakshmi  
**B)** Saraswati  
**C)** Parvati  
**D)** Durga  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Goddess Parvati is Ganesh's mother who created him from turmeric paste.

### Question 7
**Q:** Ganesh's favorite sweet is?  
**A)** Laddu  
**B)** Modak  
**C)** Jalebi  
**D)** Barfi  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Modak is Ganesh's favorite sweet, often offered during prayers.

### Question 8
**Q:** Ganesh's festival is called?  
**A)** Diwali  
**B)** Holi  
**C)** Ganesh Chaturthi  
**D)** Navratri  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesh Chaturthi is the major festival celebrating Lord Ganesh.

### Question 9
**Q:** Ganesh has how many arms usually?  
**A)** 2  
**B)** 4  
**C)** 6  
**D)** 8  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesh is commonly depicted with four arms holding various objects.

### Question 10
**Q:** Ganesh is lord of?  
**A)** Wisdom  
**B)** Wealth  
**C)** Beginnings  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesh represents wisdom, prosperity, and auspicious beginnings.

### Question 11
**Q:** Ganesh's brother is?  
**A)** Hanuman  
**B)** Kartikeya  
**C)** Indra  
**D)** Vishnu  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Kartikeya (Murugan/Skanda) is Ganesh's younger brother.

### Question 12
**Q:** Ganesh broke his tusk to write?  
**A)** Vedas  
**B)** Ramayana  
**C)** Mahabharata  
**D)** Puranas  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesh broke his tusk to use as a pen to write the Mahabharata dictated by Vyasa.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Ganesh's 108 names are called?  
**A)** Ganesha Sahasranama  
**B)** Ganesha Ashtottara  
**C)** Ganesha Chalisa  
**D)** Ganesha Stotra  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganesha Ashtottara Shatanamavali contains 108 names of Lord Ganesh.

### Question 14
**Q:** Who gave Ganesh the elephant head?  
**A)** Parvati  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Vishnu  
**D)** Brahma  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Shiva replaced Ganesh's severed head with that of an elephant.

### Question 15
**Q:** Ganesh's consorts are?  
**A)** Riddhi and Siddhi  
**B)** Lakshmi and Saraswati  
**C)** Ganga and Yamuna  
**D)** Shachi and Paulomi  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Riddhi (prosperity) and Siddhi (spiritual power) are Ganesh's wives.

### Question 16
**Q:** Ganesh Chaturthi lasts for how many days?  
**A)** 7  
**B)** 10  
**C)** 11  
**D)** 15  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganesh Chaturthi festival traditionally lasts for 11 days.

### Question 17
**Q:** Ganesh's weapon is?  
**A)** Trident  
**B)** Ankusha (goad)  
**C)** Sword  
**D)** Mace  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganesh carries an ankusha (elephant goad) to remove obstacles.

### Question 18
**Q:** Ganesh is also called?  
**A)** Ganapati  
**B)** Vinayaka  
**C)** Vighneshwara  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganesh has many names including Ganapati, Vinayaka, and Vighneshwara.

### Question 19
**Q:** Ganesh's belly represents?  
**A)** Greed  
**B)** Universe  
**C)** Prosperity  
**D)** Contentment  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganesh's large belly symbolically contains the entire universe.

### Question 20
**Q:** Ganesh was created by Parvati from?  
**A)** Clay  
**B)** Turmeric paste  
**C)** Sandalwood  
**D)** Milk  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Parvati created Ganesh from turmeric paste (ubtan) from her body.

### Question 21
**Q:** Ganesh's trunk curves to which side?  
**A)** Left  
**B)** Right  
**C)** Both  
**D)** Straight  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganesh's trunk usually curves to the left, representing the lunar path.

### Question 22
**Q:** Ganesh holds which sweet in his hand?  
**A)** Laddu  
**B)** Modak  
**C)** Both  
**D)** Neither  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganesh is often depicted holding his favorite modak in one hand.

### Question 23
**Q:** Ganesh's mantra "Om Gam" represents?  
**A)** Prosperity  
**B)** Wisdom  
**C)** Obstacle removal  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The bija mantra "Gam" encompasses all of Ganesh's powers and blessings.

### Question 24
**Q:** Ganesh immersion is called?  
**A)** Visarjan  
**B)** Aarti  
**C)** Puja  
**D)** Darshan  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Ganesh Visarjan is the ritual immersion of Ganesh idols in water.

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25
**Q:** Ganesh's mouse is named?  
**A)** Kroncha  
**B)** Mushika  
**C)** Akhu  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Ganesh's mouse vehicle has various names in different texts and traditions.

### Question 26
**Q:** Ganesh Purana has how many chapters?  
**A)** 32  
**B)** 42  
**C)** 52  
**D)** 62  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Ganesha Purana contains 32 chapters dedicated to Lord Ganesh.

### Question 27
**Q:** Ganesh's Ashtavinayak temples are in?  
**A)** Tamil Nadu  
**B)** Karnataka  
**C)** Maharashtra  
**D)** Gujarat  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The eight sacred Ashtavinayak temples are located in Maharashtra.

### Question 28
**Q:** Ganesh's single tusk symbolizes?  
**A)** Sacrifice  
**B)** Non-duality  
**C)** Wisdom  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The single tusk (ekadanta) represents sacrifice, non-duality, and focused wisdom.

### Question 29
**Q:** Ganesh Atharvashirsha belongs to which Veda?  
**A)** Rigveda  
**B)** Samaveda  
**C)** Yajurveda  
**D)** Atharvaveda  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Ganesh Atharvashirsha is a sacred text from the Atharvaveda tradition.

### Question 30
**Q:** Ganesh's form with 32 arms is called?  
**A)** Mahaganapati  
**B)** Ucchishta Ganapati  
**C)** Heramba Ganapati  
**D)** Dvimukha Ganapati  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Mahaganapati is the supreme form of Ganesh depicted with 32 arms.
