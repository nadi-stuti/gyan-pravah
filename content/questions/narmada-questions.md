## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Narmada River flows through which states primarily?  
**A)** Bihar and Jharkhand  
**B)** Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat  
**C)** Rajasthan and Punjab  
**D)** Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Narmada River flows through Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat states.

### Question 2

**A)** South India  
**B)** Central India  
**C)** India  
**D)** Asia  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Narmada is the second longest river in India after the Ganges.
