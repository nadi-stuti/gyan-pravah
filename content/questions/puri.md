# Puri - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Dham (Pilgrimage Sites)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Puri is located in which state?  
**A)** West Bengal  
**B)** Odisha  
**C)** Andhra Pradesh  
**D)** Tamil Nadu  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Puri is a coastal city in Odisha state, famous for Jagannath Temple.

### Question 2
**Q:** Puri is famous for which deity?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Shiva  
**C)** Jagannath  
**D)** Ganesha  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Puri is renowned for Lord Jagannath, a form of Krishna/Vishnu.

### Question 3
**Q:** Puri is one of the?  
**A)** Char Dham  
**B)** Jyotirlingas  
**C)** Shakti Peethas  
**D)** Panch Kedar  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Puri is one of the four sacred Char Dham pilgrimage sites.

### Question 4
**Q:** Famous festival of Puri is?  
**A)** Diwali  
**B)** Rath Yatra  
**C)** Holi  
**D)** Dussehra  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Rath Yatra (Chariot Festival) is Puri's most famous annual celebration.

### Question 5
**Q:** Puri is situated on?  
**A)** Arabian Sea  
**B)** Bay of Bengal  
**C)** Indian Ocean  
**D)** River Ganga  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Puri is a coastal city on the shores of Bay of Bengal.

### Question 6
**Q:** Jagannath Temple was built by?  
**A)** Chola dynasty  
**B)** Ganga dynasty  
**C)** Pallava dynasty  
**D)** Chalukya dynasty  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Jagannath Temple was built by King Anantavarman of Ganga dynasty.

### Question 7
**Q:** Jagannath's siblings are?  
**A)** Balabhadra and Subhadra  
**B)** Radha and Rukmini  
**C)** Lakshman and Shatrughna  
**D)** Ganesha and Kartikeya  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Lord Jagannath is worshipped with his brother Balabhadra and sister Subhadra.

### Question 8
**Q:** Puri beach is famous for?  
**A)** Golden sand  
**B)** Sunrise  
**C)** Pilgrimage  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Puri beach is renowned for its golden sand, beautiful sunrise, and religious significance.

### Question 9
**Q:** Jagannath means?  
**A)** Lord of universe  
**B)** Lord of world  
**C)** Both A & B  
**D)** Lord of devotees  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Jagannath means "Lord of the Universe" or "Lord of the World."

### Question 10
**Q:** Puri's ancient name was?  
**A)** Purushottama Puri  
**B)** Jagannath Puri  
**C)** Nilachala  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Puri has been known by various names including Purushottama Puri and Nilachala.

### Question 11
**Q:** Jagannath Temple's main dome is called?  
**A)** Shikhara  
**B)** Neelachakra  
**C)** Kalasha  
**D)** Vimana

**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The temple's main dome is topped with the sacred Neelachakra (blue wheel).

### Question 12
**Q:** Puri is connected to which other Dham?  
**A)** Dwarka  
**B)** Badrinath  
**C)** Rameswaram  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Puri is one of the four Char Dham along with Dwarka, Badrinath, and Rameswaram.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Rath Yatra chariots are called?  
**A)** Nandighosa, Taladhvaja, Darpadalana  
**B)** Garuda, Hanuman, Nandi  
**C)** Surya, Chandra, Agni  
**D)** Brahma, Vishnu, Mahesh  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The three chariots are Nandighosa (Jagannath), Taladhvaja (Balabhadra), and Darpadalana (Subhadra).

### Question 14
**Q:** Jagannath Temple height is approximately?  
**A)** 150 feet  
**B)** 200 feet  
**C)** 214 feet  
**D)** 250 feet  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Jagannath Temple stands about 214 feet tall, dominating Puri's skyline.

### Question 15
**Q:** Rath Yatra destination is?  
**A)** Gundicha Temple  
**B)** Mausi Maa Temple  
**C)** Lokanath Temple  
**D)** Markandeshwar Temple  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The chariots travel from Jagannath Temple to Gundicha Temple during Rath Yatra.

### Question 16
**Q:** Jagannath's wooden idols are replaced every?  
**A)** 5 years  
**B)** 8 years  
**C)** 12 years  
**D)** 19 years  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The wooden idols are ceremonially replaced every 12-19 years in Nabakalebara ritual.

### Question 17
**Q:** Puri's famous prasadam is?  
**A)** Mahaprasad  
**B)** Kheer  
**C)** Laddu  
**D)** Peda  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Mahaprasad from Jagannath Temple is considered extremely sacred and powerful.

### Question 18
**Q:** Jagannath Temple has how many gates?  
**A)** 2  
**B)** 3  
**C)** 4  
**D)** 5  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The temple has four gates: Singhadwara (Lion), Ashwadwara (Horse), Vyaghradwara (Tiger), and Hastidwara (Elephant).

### Question 19
**Q:** Puri's Shankaracharya Peetha was established by?  
**A)** Adi Shankaracharya  
**B)** Govinda Bhagavatpada  
**C)** Mandana Mishra  
**D)** Kumarila Bhatta  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Adi Shankaracharya established the Govardhan Math (Puri Peetha) in Puri.

### Question 20
**Q:** Jagannath's eyes are made of?  
**A)** Gold  
**B)** Silver  
**C)** Precious stones  
**D)** Special paint  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The distinctive large eyes of Jagannath are made with precious stones and special materials.

### Question 21

**A)** 7 days  
**B)** 9 days

**D)** 15 days

### Question 22

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25
**Q:** Jagannath Temple's architectural style is?  
**A)** Kalinga  
**B)** Dravidian  
**C)** Nagara  
**D)** Vesara  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The temple follows the distinctive Kalinga architectural style of Odisha.

### Question 26
**Q:** Nabakalebara ceremony occurs when?  
**A)** Every 12 years  
**B)** When Ashadha has two Purnimas  
**C)** During solar eclipse  
**D)** On Jagannath's birthday  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Nabakalebara happens when the month of Ashadha has two full moon days (Adhik Maas).

### Question 27
**Q:** Jagannath Temple was completed in?  
**A)** 1078 CE  
**B)** 1161 CE  
**C)** 1198 CE  
**D)** 1230 CE  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The temple construction was completed around 1161 CE during King Anantavarman's reign.

### Question 28
**Q:** Puri's Govardhan Math represents which direction?  
**A)** North  
**B)** South  
**C)** East  
**D)** West  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Govardhan Math in Puri represents the eastern direction in Shankaracharya's four cardinal peeth system.

### Question 29
**Q:** Jagannath's original form is believed to be?  
**A)** Nila Madhava  
**B)** Purushottama  
**C)** Neelakantha  
**D)** Nilachala  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The original deity was Nila Madhava, discovered by King Indradyumna in ancient times.

### Question 30
**Q:** Puri's spiritual significance in Gaudiya Vaishnavism comes from?  
**A)** Chaitanya Mahaprabhu's residence  
**B)** Jagannath worship tradition  
**C)** Ratha Yatra importance  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Puri holds supreme importance in Gaudiya Vaishnavism due to Chaitanya's life there and Jagannath worship.
