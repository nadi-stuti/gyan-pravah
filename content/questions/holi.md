# Holi - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Utsav (Festivals)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Holi is festival of?  
**A)** Lights  
**B)** Colors  
**C)** Sweets  
**D)** Flowers  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holi is famously known as the festival of colors, celebrating spring.

### Question 2
**Q:** Holi celebrates victory of?  
**A)** Good over evil  
**B)** Light over darkness  
**C)** Love over hate  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holi symbolizes the triumph of good over evil and arrival of spring.

### Question 3
**Q:** Holi is associated with which deity?  
**A)** Rama  
**B)** Krishna  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Ganesh  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holi is closely associated with Lord Krishna and his playful nature.

### Question 4
**Q:** Holi bonfire is called?  
**A)** Dahan  
**B)** Holika Dahan  
**C)** Agni  
**D)** Yajna  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The bonfire lit on Holi eve is called Holika Dahan.

### Question 5
**Q:** Holi falls in which month?  
**A)** Chaitra  
**B)** Phalguna  
**C)** Kartik  
**D)** Magha  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holi is celebrated on Phalguna Purnima (full moon day of Phalguna).

### Question 6
**Q:** Holi colors are called?  
**A)** Rang  
**B)** Gulal  
**C)** Abir  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holi colors are known by various names - rang, gulal, and abir.

### Question 7
**Q:** Holi lasts for how many days?  
**A)** 1  
**B)** 2  
**C)** 3  
**D)** 5  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holi is celebrated for two days - Holika Dahan and Rangwali Holi.

### Question 8
**Q:** Holi water balloons are called?  
**A)** Pichkari  
**B)** Gulal  
**C)** Balloon  
**D)** Rang  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Water guns and balloons used in Holi are called pichkari.

### Question 9
**Q:** Holi celebrates which season?  
**A)** Winter  
**B)** Summer  
**C)** Spring  
**D)** Monsoon  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holi marks the arrival of spring season in India.

### Question 10
**Q:** Holika was sister of?  
**A)** Ravana  
**B)** Hiranyakashipu  
**C)** Kamsa  
**D)** Mahishasura  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holika was the sister of demon king Hiranyakashipu.

### Question 11
**Q:** Holi is also called?  
**A)** Phagwah  
**B)** Dol Jatra  
**C)** Basant Utsav  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Holi has different regional names like Phagwah, Dol Jatra, and Basant Utsav.

### Question 12
**Q:** Traditional Holi sweet is?  
**A)** Laddu  
**B)** Gujiya  
**C)** Barfi  
**D)** Jalebi  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Gujiya is the traditional sweet prepared and shared during Holi.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13

**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** His devotion  
**C)** Divine intervention  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Prahlada's devotion to Vishnu saved him when Holika tried to burn him.

### Question 14

**A)** 7 days  
**B)** 10 days  
**C)** 15 days  
**D)** 16 days  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 15

**A)** Mathura  
**B)** Barsana  
**C)** Vrindavan  
**D)** Gokul  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Lathmar Holi, where women playfully beat men with sticks, is famous in Barsana.

### Question 16

**B)** Holika-Prahlada  
**C)** Radha-Krishna love  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 17

**A)** Turmeric

**D)** All above
