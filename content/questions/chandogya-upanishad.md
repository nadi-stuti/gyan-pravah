# Chandogya Upanishad - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Shruti (Vedic Scriptures)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Chandogya Upanishad belongs to which Veda?  
**A)** Rigveda  
**B)** Samaveda  
**C)** Yajurveda  
**D)** Atharvaveda  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Chandogya Upanishad is part of the Samaveda tradition.

### Question 2
**Q:** Chandogya Upanishad has how many chapters?  
**A)** 6  
**B)** 7  
**C)** 8  
**D)** 9  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Chandogya Upanishad contains eight chapters (adhyayas).

### Question 3
**Q:** Famous teaching "Tat tvam asi" appears in?  
**A)** Chandogya Upanishad  
**B)** Brihadaranyaka  
**C)** Kena Upanishad  
**D)** Isa Upanishad  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** "Tat tvam asi" (Thou art That) is a key teaching from Chandogya Upanishad.

### Question 4
**Q:** "Tat tvam asi" means?  
**A)** You are God  
**B)** Thou art That  
**C)** You are Brahman  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** "Tat tvam asi" means "Thou art That," indicating the identity of individual soul with Brahman.

### Question 5
**Q:** Chandogya Upanishad emphasizes?  
**A)** Meditation  
**B)** Knowledge  
**C)** Om chanting  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Upanishad teaches meditation, self-knowledge, and the significance of Om.

### Question 6
**Q:** The teacher Uddalaka taught?  
**A)** Aruni  
**B)** Svetaketu  
**C)** Nachiketa  
**D)** Satyakama  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sage Uddalaka Aruni taught his son Svetaketu the profound truths of Vedanta.

### Question 7
**Q:** Chandogya discusses the nature of?  
**A)** Atman  
**B)** Brahman  
**C)** Reality  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The Upanishad explores the nature of Atman (soul), Brahman (ultimate reality), and existence.

### Question 8
**Q:** "Sarvam khalvidam brahma" means?  
**A)** All this is Brahman  
**B)** Brahman is everywhere  
**C)** Everything is divine  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** This famous statement declares that all existence is Brahman (ultimate reality).

### Question 9

**A)** Sanskrit  
**B)** Pali

**D)** Tamil

**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Like all Upanishads, Chandogya is composed in Sanskrit.

### Question 10

**A)** Self-realization  
**B)** Ultimate truth  
**C)** Spiritual knowledge  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 11
**Q:** Om is described as?  
**A)** Sacred sound  
**B)** Cosmic vibration  
**C)** Brahman symbol  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 12
**Q:** Chandogya belongs to which category of texts?  
**A)** Shruti  
**B)** Smriti  
**C)** Purana  
**D)** Itihasa

**C)** Inner self  
**D)** All above

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 21

### Question 28

**A)** Sat-karya-vada

### Question 30

**A)** Monistic  
**B)** Dualistic
