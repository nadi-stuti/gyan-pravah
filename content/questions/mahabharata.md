# Mahabharata - Quiz Questions

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Who wrote Mahabharata?  
**A)** Valmiki  
**B)** Vyasa  
**C)** Tulsidas  
**D)** Kalidasa  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sage Vyasa wrote the Mahabharata.

### Question 2
**Q:** How many Pandavas were there?  
**A)** 3  
**B)** 4  
**C)** 5  
**D)** 6  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** There were five Pandava brothers: Yudhishthira, Bhima, Arjuna, Nakula, and Sahadeva.

### Question 3
**Q:** Kurukshetra war lasted for?  
**A)** 16 days  
**B)** 17 days  
**C)** 18 days  
**D)** 19 days  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The great Kurukshetra war lasted for 18 days.

### Question 4
**Q:** Who was the eldest Pandava?  
**A)** Arjuna  
**B)** Bhima  
**C)** Yudhishthira  
**D)** Nakula  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Yudhishthira was the eldest of the five Pandava brothers.

### Question 5
**Q:** How many Kauravas were there?  
**A)** 50  
**B)** 75  
**C)** 100  
**D)** 125  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** There were 100 Kaurava brothers led by Duryodhana.

### Question 6
**Q:** Draupadi was wife of?  
**A)** Arjuna only  
**B)** All 5 Pandavas  
**C)** Yudhishthira only  
**D)** Bhima only  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Draupadi was the common wife of all five Pandava brothers.

### Question 7

**A)** Father  
**B)** Brother  
**C)** Uncle  
**D)** Cousin & guide  
**Correct Answer:** D

**B)** Dushasana

**D)** Karna

**Difficulty:** Easy

**A)** Celibacy  
**B)** Silence

**D)** Truth

**A)** Pinaka

### Question 12

**A)** 12  
**B)** 13  
**C)** 14  
**D)** 15
