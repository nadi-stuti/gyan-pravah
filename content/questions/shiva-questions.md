# Shiva - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Bhagvan (Deities)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Shiva's consort is?  
**A)** Lakshmi  
**B)** Saraswati  
**C)** Parvati  
**D)** Sita  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Parvati (also known as Shakti, Uma, Gauri) is Shiva's consort.

### Question 2
**Q:** Shiva's vehicle is?  
**A)** Garuda  
**B)** Nandi  
**C)** Peacock  
**D)** Mouse  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Nandi, the sacred bull, is Lord Shiva's vehicle.

### Question 3
**Q:** Shiva has how many eyes?  
**A)** Two  
**B)** Three  
**C)** Four  
**D)** Five  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Shiva is called Trinetra (three-eyed), with the third eye on his forehead.

### Question 4
**Q:** Shiva's abode is?  
**A)** Vaikuntha  
**B)** Kailash  
**C)** Brahmaloka  
**D)** Indraloka  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Mount Kailash is the divine abode of Lord Shiva.

### Question 5
**Q:** Shiva holds which weapon?  
**A)** Chakra  
**B)** Trishul  
**C)** Gada  
**D)** Bow  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Trishul (trident) is Shiva's primary weapon.

### Question 6
**Q:** Shiva's sons are?  
**A)** Ganesha  
**B)** Kartikeya  
**C)** Both A & B  
**D)** Neither  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganesha and Kartikeya (Skanda/Murugan) are Shiva's sons.

### Question 7
**Q:** Shiva wears which serpent?  
**A)** Shesha  
**B)** Vasuki  
**C)** Takshaka  
**D)** Kaliya  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vasuki, the king of serpents, is worn by Shiva around his neck.

### Question 8
**Q:** Shiva's dance is called?  
**A)** Ras Leela  
**B)** Tandava  
**C)** Kathak  
**D)** Bharatanatyam  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Tandava is Shiva's cosmic dance of creation and destruction.

### Question 9
**Q:** Ganga flows from whose hair?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Brahma  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Ganga flows from Shiva's matted locks (jata).

### Question 10
**Q:** Shiva's throat is which color?  
**A)** Red  
**B)** Blue  
**C)** Green  
**D)** White  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Shiva is called Neelakantha (blue-throated) after drinking poison.

### Question 11
**Q:** Shiva's third eye opens when?  
**A)** Happy  
**B)** Sad  
**C)** Angry  
**D)** Meditating  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Shiva's third eye opens in anger and can destroy with its gaze.

### Question 12
**Q:** Most sacred mantra for Shiva?  
**A)** Om  
**B)** Om Namah Shivaya  
**C)** Hari Om  
**D)** Om Namo Bhagavate  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** "Om Namah Shivaya" is the five-syllabled sacred mantra of Shiva.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Shiva destroyed which demon's fortress?  
**A)** Tripurasura  
**B)** Ravana  
**C)** Hiranyakashipu  
**D)** Mahishasura  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Shiva destroyed the three cities (Tripura) of demon Tripurasura.

### Question 14
**Q:** How many Jyotirlingas are there?  
**A)** 8  
**B)** 10  
**C)** 12  
**D)** 16  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** There are 12 Jyotirlingas, the most sacred Shiva temples.

### Question 15
**Q:** Shiva's bow is named?  
**A)** Gandiva  
**B)** Pinaka  
**C)** Sharanga  
**D)** Vijaya  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Pinaka is the divine bow of Lord Shiva.

### Question 16
**Q:** Which moon adorns Shiva's head?  
**A)** Full moon  
**B)** New moon  
**C)** Crescent  
**D)** Half moon  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** A crescent moon adorns Shiva's matted hair.

### Question 17
**Q:** Shiva saved world from which poison?  
**A)** Garuda  
**B)** Halahala  
**C)** Vasuki  
**D)** Kalakuta  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Shiva drank Halahala/Kalakuta poison during Samudra Manthan.

### Question 18
**Q:** Shiva is called Mahadev meaning?  
**A)** Great dancer  
**B)** Great god  
**C)** Great warrior  
**D)** Great teacher  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Mahadev means "Great God" or "God of all gods."

### Question 19
**Q:** Shiva represents which guna primarily?  
**A)** Sattvaguna  
**B)** Rajoguna  
**C)** Tamoguna  
**D)** All three  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Shiva transcends all three gunas but embodies all aspects.

### Question 20
**Q:** Which Veda mentions Rudra (Shiva)?  
**A)** Rigveda  
**B)** Samaveda  
**C)** Yajurveda  
**D)** All Vedas  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rudra (early form of Shiva) is mentioned in all four Vedas.

### Question 21
**Q:** Shiva's damaru (drum) symbolizes?  
**A)** Music  
**B)** Creation  
**C)** Time  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The damaru symbolizes the rhythm of creation, music, and time.

### Question 22
**Q:** Shiva meditated for how many years?  
**A)** 1,000  
**B)** 10,000  
**C)** Countless  
**D)** 100  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Shiva meditates for countless ages in deep samadhi.

### Question 23
**Q:** Which river besides Ganga is in Shiva's hair?  
**A)** Yamuna  
**B)** Saraswati  
**C)** Narmada  
**D)** Godavari  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** According to some texts, Saraswati also flows from Shiva's locks.

### Question 24
**Q:** Shiva's form as destroyer is called?  
**A)** Rudra  
**B)** Bhairava  
**C)** Mahakala  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Rudra, Bhairava, and Mahakala are all destroyer forms of Shiva.

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25
**Q:** How many Rudras are mentioned in Vedas?  
**A)** 7  
**B)** 11  
**C)** 33  
**D)** 108  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Vedas mention 11 Rudras, aspects of Shiva.

### Question 26
**Q:** Shiva Purana has how many Samhitas?  
**A)** 3  
**B)** 5  
**C)** 7  
**D)** 12  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Shiva Purana traditionally has 7 Samhitas (sections).

### Question 27
**Q:** Om Namah Shivaya has how many syllables?  
**A)** 3  
**B)** 5  
**C)** 7  
**D)** 8  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The Panchakshara mantra has 5 syllables: Na-mah-Shi-va-ya.

### Question 28
**Q:** Nataraja form represents Shiva as?  
**A)** Meditator  
**B)** Cosmic dancer  
**C)** Warrior  
**D)** Ascetic  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Nataraja is Shiva's form as the cosmic dancer performing Tandava.

### Question 29
**Q:** Ardhanarishvara represents?  
**A)** Shiva alone  
**B)** Parvati alone  
**C)** Half Shiva, half Parvati  
**D)** Shiva family  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Ardhanarishvara is the combined form of Shiva and Shakti (Parvati).

### Question 30
**Q:** Shiva's form with five faces is called?  
**A)** Panchanan  
**B)** Panchamukha  
**C)** Sadashiva  
**D)** All above  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Panchanan, Panchamukha, and Sadashiva all refer to Shiva's five-faced form.
