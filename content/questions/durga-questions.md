# Durga - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Bhagvan (Deities)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Durga is also known as?  
**A)** Lakshmi  
**B)** Shakti  
**C)** Saraswati  
**D)** Sita  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga is the manifestation of Shakti, the divine feminine power.

### Question 2
**Q:** Durga rides which animal?  
**A)** Elephant  
**B)** Horse  
**C)** Lion or Tiger  
**D)** Peacock  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Goddess Durga rides a lion or tiger as her vahana.

### Question 3
**Q:** Durga has how many arms?  
**A)** 4  
**B)** 6  
**C)** 8  
**D)** 10  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga is typically depicted with 8 or 10 arms holding different weapons.

### Question 4
**Q:** Which demon did Durga kill?  
**A)** Ravana  
**B)** Mahishasura  
**C)** Hiranyakashipu  
**D)** Kansa  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga killed the buffalo demon Mahishasura.

### Question 5
**Q:** Durga Puja is celebrated in which month?  
**A)** Chaitra  
**B)** Ashwin  
**C)** Kartik  
**D)** Magha  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga Puja is celebrated in Ashwin month (September-October).

### Question 6
**Q:** Durga is consort of?  
**A)** Vishnu  
**B)** Brahma  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga/Parvati is the consort of Lord Shiva.

### Question 7
**Q:** Durga symbolizes victory of?  
**A)** Good over evil  
**B)** Light over dark  
**C)** Truth over lie  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga represents the triumph of good, light, and truth.

### Question 8
**Q:** Durga Saptashati has how many verses?  
**A)** 500  
**B)** 700  
**C)** 900  
**D)** 1000  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Devi Mahatmyam (Durga Saptashati) contains 700 verses.

### Question 9
**Q:** Which scripture describes Durga's glory?  
**A)** Devi Mahatmyam  
**B)** Durga Saptashati  
**C)** Chandi Path  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** All these names refer to the same scripture glorifying Durga.

### Question 10
**Q:** Durga holds which weapons?  
**A)** Trishul  
**B)** Chakra  
**C)** Sword  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga carries various weapons including trishul, chakra, sword, and more.

### Question 11
**Q:** Durga Puja lasts for how many days?  
**A)** 3  
**B)** 5  
**C)** 7  
**D)** 9  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga Puja is celebrated for nine days during Navratri.

### Question 12
**Q:** Durga is especially worshipped in?  
**A)** Bengal  
**B)** Assam  
**C)** Odisha  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Durga Puja is a major festival in Bengal, Assam, and Odisha.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Mahishasura could be killed only by?  
**A)** Man  
**B)** Woman  
**C)** God  
**D)** Demon  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Mahishasura had a boon that he couldn't be killed by any man or god.

### Question 14
**Q:** Durga was created by combining powers of?  
**A)** Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva  
**B)** All devas  
**C)** Trinity  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** All gods combined their powers to create Durga to defeat Mahishasura.

### Question 15
**Q:** Nine forms of Durga are called?  
**A)** Navaratri  
**B)** Navadurga  
**C)** Navagraha  
**D)** Navadevi  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The nine forms of Durga worshipped during Navratri are called Navadurga.

### Question 16
**Q:** Which day is Durga's final victory?  
**A)** Saptami  
**B)** Ashtami  
**C)** Navami  
**D)** Dashami  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Vijayadashami (tenth day) marks Durga's victory over Mahishasura.

### Question 17
**Q:** Durga emerged from?  
**A)** Ocean  
**B)** Fire  
**C)** Combined light of gods  
**D)** Earth  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Durga emerged from the combined tejas (light/energy) of all gods.

### Question 18
**Q:** Durga's third eye represents?  
**A)** Wisdom  
**B)** Knowledge  
**C)** Divine vision  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The third eye symbolizes wisdom, knowledge, and spiritual insight.

### Question 19
**Q:** Which Purana describes Durga extensively?  
**A)** Markandeya Purana  
**B)** Vishnu Purana  
**C)** Shiva Purana  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Markandeya Purana contains the Devi Mahatmyam, the primary text on Durga.

### Question 20
**Q:** Durga's battle lasted for how many days?  
**A)** 3  
**B)** 7  
**C)** 9  
**D)** 10  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The battle between Durga and Mahishasura lasted nine days.

### Question 21
**Q:** Kumari Puja involves worshipping?  
**A)** Young girls  
**B)** Idols  
**C)** Weapons  
**D)** Nature  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Kumari Puja involves worshipping young girls as embodiments of the Goddess.

### Question 22
**Q:** Sindoor Khela is played on?  
**A)** Navami  
**B)** Dashami  
**C)** Ekadashi  
**D)** Purnima  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Sindoor Khela (vermillion play) is performed on Vijayadashami.

### Question 23
**Q:** Durga's Trishul was given by?  
**A)** Brahma  
**B)** Vishnu  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Indra  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Lord Shiva gave his trident (Trishul) to Durga.

### Question 24
**Q:** Durga's lion represents?  
**A)** Strength  
**B)** Courage  
**C)** Determination  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The lion symbolizes strength, courage, and unwavering determination.

## Hard Questions (25-30)

### Question 25
**Q:** Devi Mahatmyam is part of which Purana?  
**A)** Skanda Purana  
**B)** Markandeya Purana  
**C)** Padma Purana  
**D)** Brahma Purana  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Devi Mahatmyam (Durga Saptashati) is chapters 81-93 of Markandeya Purana.

### Question 26
**Q:** How many chapters in Devi Mahatmyam?  
**A)** 11  
**B)** 13  
**C)** 15  
**D)** 17  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Devi Mahatmyam consists of 13 chapters.

### Question 27
**Q:** Mahishasura's name means?  
**A)** Buffalo demon  
**B)** Great demon  
**C)** Powerful demon  
**D)** Dark demon  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Mahishasura means "buffalo demon" (mahisha = buffalo).

### Question 28
**Q:** Estimated period of Devi Mahatmyam composition?  
**A)** 300 BCE  
**B)** 400-600 CE  
**C)** 800 CE  
**D)** 1000 CE  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Scholars date Devi Mahatmyam to approximately 400-600 CE.

### Question 29
**Q:** First form of Navadurga is?  
**A)** Shailputri  
**B)** Brahmacharini  
**C)** Chandraghanta  
**D)** Kushmanda  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Shailputri (daughter of the mountain) is the first form of Navadurga.

### Question 30
**Q:** Durga's abhaya mudra signifies?  
**A)** Fearlessness  
**B)** Protection  
**C)** Both Fearlessness & Protection  
**D)** Victory  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** The abhaya mudra (gesture of fearlessness) offers protection and dispels fear.
