## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Badrinath is dedicated to?  
**A)** Shiva  
**B)** Vishnu  
**C)** Brahma  
**D)** Ganesha  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Badrinath temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu as Badrinath.

### Question 2
**Q:** Badrinath is located in?  
**A)** HP  
**B)** UK  
**C)** JK  
**D)** Sikkim  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Badrinath is in Chamoli district of Uttarakhand.

### Question 3
**Q:** Badrinath temple opens in?  
**A)** March  
**B)** April  
**C)** May  
**D)** June  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The temple typically opens in late April or early May.

### Question 4
**Q:** Badrinath is part of?  
**A)** Char Dham  
**B)** Panch Kedar  
**C)** Saptapuri  
**D)** Jyotirlinga  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Badrinath is one of the four Char Dham pilgrimage sites.

### Question 5
**Q:** Which river flows near Badrinath?  
**A)** Ganga  
**B)** Yamuna  
**C)** Alaknanda  
**D)** Bhagirathi  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Alaknanda River flows near Badrinath temple.

### Question 6
**Q:** Badrinath is at altitude of?  
**A)** 2,133 m  
**B)** 3,133 m  
**C)** 4,133 m  
**D)** 5,133 m  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Badrinath temple is at an altitude of 3,133 meters (10,279 feet).

### Question 7
**Q:** Badrinath head priest is called?  
**A)** Nambudiri  
**B)** Brahmin  
**C)** Rawal  
**D)** Pujari  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The chief priest of Badrinath is called Rawal.

### Question 8
**Q:** Best time to visit Badrinath?  
**A)** Winter  
**B)** Monsoon  
**C)** Summer  
**D)** All year  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** May to June and September to October are best times to visit.

### Question 9
**Q:** Badrinath deity is made of?  
**A)** Stone  
**B)** Metal  
**C)** Wood  
**D)** Shaligram  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 10

**A)** Tapt Kund  
**B)** Surya Kund  
**C)** Chandra Kund  
**D)** Agni Kund  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 11
**Q:** Badrinath closes on?  
**A)** Bhai Dooj  
**B)** Govardhan  
**C)** Diwali  
**D)** Kartik Purnima  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The temple closes for winter on Vijaya Dashami or Bhai Dooj.

### Question 12

**A)** First village  
**B)** Last Indian village  
**C)** Oldest village  
**D)** Largest village

**Difficulty:** Easy

## Hard Questions (25-30)

**A)** Rishikesh  
**B)** Joshimath  
**C)** Haridwar

**Difficulty:** Hard
