## Question Guidelines for Development

### Answer Option Length Rules:

- **Maximum 4-5 words per option**
- Use abbreviations where appropriate (HP = Himachal Pradesh)
- Numbers should be clear and distinct
- Avoid complex compound words

### Difficulty Distribution:

- **Easy (40%)**: Basic facts, popular knowledge, obvious associations
- **Medium (40%)**: Specific details, numerical facts, connections
- **Hard (20%)**: Advanced knowledge, precise numbers, scholarly details

### Question Types by Difficulty:

**Easy Questions Focus On:**

- Basic associations (Krishna - Mathura)
- Popular festivals and their meanings
- Famous stories everyone knows
- Simple geographical facts
- Well-known characteristics

**Medium Questions Focus On:**

- Specific numbers and dates
- Lesser-known but important details
- Connections between concepts
- Regional variations
- Historical context

**Hard Questions Focus On:**

- Precise measurements and statistics
- Scholarly knowledge
- Ancient texts and sources
- Advanced philosophical concepts
- Detailed chronology

### Content Strategy:

1. **Popular First**: Start with subtopics every Indian knows
2. **Visual Appeal**: Include topics that can have good hero images
3. **Seasonal Relevance**: Festival topics align with calendar
4. **Regional Balance**: Include pan-Indian topics
5. **Age Appropriate**: Content suitable for ages 13-65
