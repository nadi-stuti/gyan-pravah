## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Swami Vivekananda's original name was?  
**A)** Narendranath Datta  
**B)** Gadadhar Chattopadhyay  
**C)** Ramakrishna Paramahamsa  
**D)** Mahendranath Gupta  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Swami Vivekananda was born as Narendranath Datta in Calcutta.

### Question 2
**Q:** Vivekananda's guru was?  
**A)** Ramana Maharshi  
**B)** Ramakrishna Paramahamsa  
**C)** Dayananda Saraswati  
**D)** Brahmananda  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa was Vivekananda's spiritual guru and guide.

### Question 3
**Q:** Vivekananda represented Hinduism at?  
**A)** World Parliament of Religions  
**B)** Indian National Congress  
**C)** Theosophical Society  
**D)** Brahmo Samaj  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Vivekananda represented Hinduism at the World Parliament of Religions in Chicago (1893).

### Question 4

**A)** Arya Samaj  
**B)** Brahmo Samaj  
**C)** Ramakrishna Mission  
**D)** Theosophical Society

**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 5

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13

**A)** Vishwanath Datta  
**B)** Bhuvaneshwari Devi  
**C)** Ramakrishna  
**D)** Keshab Sen  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Vishwanath Datta, a lawyer and social reformer, was Vivekananda's father.

### Question 14
**Q:** Vivekananda's education was at?  
**A)** Presidency College  
**B)** Scottish Church College  
**C)** General Assembly's Institution  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Vivekananda studied at General Assembly's Institution (now Scottish Church College).

### Question 15
**Q:** Vivekananda's first meeting with Ramakrishna was in?  
**A)** 1881  
**B)** 1882  
**C)** 1883  
**D)** 1884  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Narendra first met Sri Ramakrishna at Dakshineswar in November 1881.

### Question 16
**Q:** Vivekananda's wandering years as monk were?  
**A)** 1886-1893  
**B)** 1885-1892  
**C)** 1887-1894  
**D)** 1888-1895  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** After Ramakrishna's death in 1886, Vivekananda wandered across India until 1893.

### Question 17
**Q:** Vivekananda received the name "Vivekananda" at?  
**A)** Khetri  
**B)** Alwar  
**C)** Jaipur  
**D)** Ajmer  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The Maharaja of Khetri gave him the name "Vivekananda" meaning "bliss of discerning wisdom."

### Question 18
**Q:** Vivekananda's Chicago speech was on?

**B)** September 27, 1893  
**C)** Both dates  
**D)** October 15, 1893  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** He spoke on both September 11 (opening) and September 27 (closing) at the Parliament.

### Question 19

**A)** New York

**C)** London  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** He established Vedanta Societies in New York, San Francisco, London, and other cities.

### Question 20

**A)** 1896

**C)** 1898  
**D)** 1899  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 21

**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** "Daridra Narayana" represents seeing God in the poor and serving them as divine manifestations.

**D)** All options
