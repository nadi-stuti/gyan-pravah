## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Krishna River originates in which state?  
**A)** Karnataka  
**B)** Maharashtra  
**C)** Andhra Pradesh  
**D)** Tamil Nadu

**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Krishna River originates near Mahabaleshwar in Maharashtra.
