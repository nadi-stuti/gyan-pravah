# Navratri - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Utsav (Festivals)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

---

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Navratri lasts for how many days?  
**A)** 7  
**B)** 8  
**C)** 9  
**D)** 10  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Navratri means "nine nights" and is celebrated for nine days.

### Question 2
**Q:** Navratri is dedicated to?  
**A)** Lakshmi  
**B)** Saraswati  
**C)** Durga  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Navratri is primarily dedicated to Goddess Durga and her nine forms.

### Question 3
**Q:** How many Navratris are celebrated yearly?  
**A)** 2  
**B)** 3  
**C)** 4  
**D)** 5  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Four Navratris are celebrated: Chaitra, Ashadha, Sharad, and Magha.

### Question 4
**Q:** Most popular Navratri is?  
**A)** Chaitra  
**B)** Sharad  
**C)** Ashadha  
**D)** Magha  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sharad Navratri (autumn) is the most widely celebrated Navratri.

### Question 5
**Q:** Navratri dance from Gujarat is?  
**A)** Bharatanatyam  
**B)** Garba  
**C)** Kathak  
**D)** Odissi  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Garba and Dandiya are traditional Gujarati dances performed during Navratri.

### Question 6
**Q:** Navratri ends with?  
**A)** Diwali  
**B)** Dussehra  
**C)** Karva Chauth  
**D)** Dhanteras  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sharad Navratri culminates with Dussehra on the tenth day (Vijayadashami).

### Question 7
**Q:** During Navratri, devotees observe?  
**A)** Fasting  
**B)** Prayer  
**C)** Dancing  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Navratri involves fasting, prayers, dancing, and various devotional activities.

### Question 8
**Q:** First day of Navratri honors?  
**A)** Shailputri  
**B)** Brahmacharini  
**C)** Chandraghanta  
**D)** Kushmanda  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** The first day of Navratri is dedicated to Goddess Shailputri.

### Question 9
**Q:** Navratri colors have?  
**A)** Religious significance  
**B)** Astrological meaning  
**C)** Cultural importance  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Each day of Navratri has specific colors with religious and cultural significance.

### Question 10
**Q:** Kanya Pujan is performed on?  
**A)** First day  
**B)** Fifth day  
**C)** Eighth day  
**D)** Last day  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Kanya Pujan (worship of young girls) is traditionally done on Ashtami (eighth day).

### Question 11
**Q:** Navratri celebrates victory of?  
**A)** Good over evil  
**B)** Durga over Mahishasura  
**C)** Light over darkness  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Navratri celebrates Durga's victory over evil, symbolizing triumph of good.

### Question 12
**Q:** Garba dance is performed around?  
**A)** Fire  
**B)** Earthen pot  
**C)** Idol  
**D)** Tree  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Traditional Garba is danced around a clay pot (garbo) with a lamp inside.

## Medium Questions (13-24)

### Question 13
**Q:** Nine forms of Durga are called?  
**A)** Navadurga  
**B)** Navashakti  
**C)** Navadevi  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The nine forms of Goddess Durga worshipped during Navratri are called Navadurga.

### Question 14
**Q:** Chaitra Navratri begins with?  
**A)** New Year  
**B)** Spring season  
**C)** Ram Navami  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Chaitra Navratri marks Hindu New Year, spring season, and culminates in Ram Navami.

### Question 15
**Q:** Dandiya sticks represent?  
**A)** Swords  
**B)** Radha-Krishna  
**C)** Musical instruments  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Dandiya sticks symbolically represent Radha and Krishna in the dance.

### Question 16
**Q:** Navratri fasting excludes?  
**A)** Grains  
**B)** Onion-garlic  
**C)** Non-vegetarian food  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Navratri fasting typically excludes grains, onion, garlic, and non-vegetarian food.

### Question 17
**Q:** Goddess Kushmanda is associated with?  
**A)** Sun  
**B)** Moon  
**C)** Universe creation  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Kushmanda is believed to have created the universe with her divine smile.

### Question 18
**Q:** Navratri Golu is tradition of?  
**A)** Tamil Nadu  
**B)** Karnataka  
**C)** Andhra Pradesh  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Golu (doll display) during Navratri is popular in South Indian states.

### Question 19
**Q:** Sharad Navratri falls in?  
**A)** September-October  
**B)** October-November  
**C)** March-April  
**D)** June-July  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Sharad Navratri is celebrated in Ashwin month (September-October).

### Question 20
**Q:** Durga Saptashati has how many chapters?  
**A)** 7  
**B)** 13  
**C)** 21  
**D)** 108  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Durga Saptashati (Devi Mahatmya) contains 13 chapters with 700 verses.

### Question 21
**Q:** Navratri's regional name in Bengal is?  
**A)** Durga Puja  
**B)** Kali Puja  
**C)** Devi Paksha  
**D)** Mahalaya  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** In Bengal, Navratri is celebrated as the grand Durga Puja festival.

### Question 22
**Q:** Goddess Katyayani represents?  
**A)** Warrior aspect

**C)** Unmarried girls' prayers  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Katyayani is the warrior goddess who destroys evil and blesses unmarried girls.

### Question 23
**Q:** Navratri Kalash contains?  
**A)** Water  
**B)** Coconut  
**C)** Mango leaves  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** The sacred Kalash is filled with water, topped with coconut and mango leaves.

### Question 24
**Q:** Mysore Dasara is famous for?  
**A)** Royal procession  
**B)** Elephant parade  
**C)** Cultural programs  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium
