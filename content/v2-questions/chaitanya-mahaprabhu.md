# Chaitanya Mahaprabhu - Quiz Questions

**Category:** Sant (Saints)  
**Total Questions:** 30  
**Difficulty Distribution:** 12 Easy, 12 Medium, 6 Hard

## Easy Questions (1-12)

### Question 1
**Q:** Chaitanya Mahaprabhu was born in?  
**A)** Vrindavan  
**B)** Navadvip  
**C)** Puri  
**D)** Mayapur  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Chaitanya Mahaprabhu was born in Navadvip (Nadia), West Bengal.

### Question 2
**Q:** Chaitanya's original name was?  
**A)** Vishvambhar  
**B)** Nimai  
**C)** Both Vishvambhar & Nimai  
**D)** Gauranga  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** He was named Vishvambhar at birth but was commonly called Nimai.

### Question 3
**Q:** Chaitanya was devoted to?  
**A)** Rama  
**B)** Krishna  
**C)** Shiva  
**D)** Vishnu  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Chaitanya Mahaprabhu was a passionate devotee of Lord Krishna.

### Question 4
**Q:** Chaitanya lived in which century?  
**A)** 14th  
**B)** 15th  
**C)** 16th  
**D)** 17th  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Chaitanya Mahaprabhu lived in the 16th century (1486-1534 CE).

### Question 5
**Q:** Chaitanya popularized?  
**A)** Hare Krishna mantra  
**B)** Sankirtana  
**C)** Bhakti movement  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** He popularized the Hare Krishna mantra, sankirtana (congregational chanting), and bhakti.

### Question 6
**Q:** Chaitanya's philosophy is called?  
**A)** Advaita  
**B)** Dvaita  
**C)** Achintya Bhedabheda  
**D)** Vishishtadvaita  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** His philosophy is Achintya Bhedabheda (inconceivable difference and non-difference).

### Question 7
**Q:** Chaitanya took sannyasa at age?  
**A)** 22  
**B)** 24  
**C)** 26  
**D)** 28  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Chaitanya took sannyasa (renunciation) at the young age of 24.

### Question 8
**Q:** Chaitanya spent his later years in?  
**A)** Navadvip  
**B)** Vrindavan  
**C)** Puri  
**D)** Mayapur  
**Correct Answer:** C  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Chaitanya spent the last 18 years of his life in Puri, Odisha.

### Question 9
**Q:** Chaitanya's mother was?  
**A)** Sachi Devi  
**B)** Vishnu Priya  
**C)** Lakshmi Priya  
**D)** Ganga Devi  
**Correct Answer:** A  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Sachi Devi was Chaitanya's devoted mother who supported his spiritual mission.

### Question 10
**Q:** Chaitanya is considered avatar of?  
**A)** Krishna  
**B)** Radha-Krishna  
**C)** Vishnu  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** B  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Explanation:** Followers believe Chaitanya is the combined avatar of Radha and Krishna.

### Question 11
**Q:** Chaitanya's movement emphasized?  
**A)** Love devotion  
**B)** Congregational chanting  
**C)** Emotional bhakti  
**D)** All options  
**Correct Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 12
**Q:** Chaitanya's famous ecstatic states were called?  
**A)** Bhava  
**B)** Mahabhava

**D)** All options

**Difficulty:** Easy
